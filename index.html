George "Buzz" Beurling: The Falcon Of Malta

The Canadian Ace and Malta Defender

The Early Life Of George Beurling.

George Beurling was born in Verdun, Quebec, in 1921. He was passionate about flying planes from a decently young age and became known for his strong vision and shooting ability.

Joining The Royal Air Force

During 1942, Beurling joined the Royal Air Force and was sent to defend malta a greatly needed Allied base in the mediterranean

The Heroism In Malta

Known for his fearless tatics and accurate shooting beurling took down 27 enemy aircrafts in Malta. His skills and bravery made him a known legend over malta till this day.
